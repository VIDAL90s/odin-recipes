Feijoada

Ever since I was a little kid, I always loved Feijoada. My mother used to make it a lot on winter because its a cosy dish!

Feijoada (Portuguese pronunciation: [fejʒuˈadɐ]) is a stew of beans with beef and pork. The name feijoada comes from feijão, 'bean' in Portuguese. It is widely prepared in the Portuguese-speaking world, with slight variations. Feijoada. Brazilian-style feijoada with common side dishes.
